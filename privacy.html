Privacy Policy

Effective June 1, 2026  ·  Version 1.0  ·  MusiGod Publishing Administration Platform
Version 1.0 — Effective June 1, 2026
Your data is used only to administer your publishing rights and recover your royalties. MusiGod does not sell personal data. We do not use your catalog information for any purpose other than the services you have authorized.

1. Who We Are

MusiGod is a publishing administration platform operated from Detroit, Michigan. We provide royalty recovery, rights registration, and catalog administration services for independent artists and rights holders.

This Privacy Policy applies to all data collected through musigod.com, the artist portal, the rights audit flow, the registration process, and all associated administrative workflows.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to provide publishing administration services:

Identity and Contact Data

  • Legal name, stage name, email address, phone number
  • Business entity information (LLC name, EIN if provided)
  • PRO membership IDs (ASCAP, BMI, SESAC, SoundExchange, etc.)

Catalog and Rights Data

  • Song titles, ISRC codes, ISWC codes, release dates
  • Publisher credits, co-writer splits, catalog metadata
  • Copyright registration numbers
  • Streaming and distribution data provided by you

Financial and Recovery Data

  • Royalty statements and payment history you upload
  • Recovery estimates and audit findings generated by our systems
  • Stripe payment records (processed by Stripe; MusiGod does not store card details)
  • Recovery case status and disbursement records

Uploaded Documents

  • Publishing agreements, split sheets, copyright certificates
  • Royalty statements and distribution reports
  • Identification documents (if required for entity formation)
  • Any other documents you submit through the platform

Operational and Technical Data

  • IP addresses at registration, login, and agreement signing
  • Browser and device information (user agent)
  • Timestamps of key platform actions
  • Audit findings, operational logs, and administrative notes
  • Agreement acceptance and signature events

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Administer your publishing catalog and rights registrations
  • Identify uncollected and under-collected royalties
  • Conduct recovery operations with collection societies on your behalf
  • Generate audit reports, recovery estimates, and operational status updates
  • Fulfill agreements you have signed with MusiGod
  • Communicate with you about your account, recovery status, and required actions
  • Comply with legal obligations, including document retention requirements
  • Maintain security, fraud prevention, and operational integrity

We do not use your catalog data for advertising. We do not sell, license, or share your personal or catalog data with third parties except as described in this policy.

5. Uploaded Documents and Statements

Documents you upload are stored in our secure database (Supabase) and are accessible only to MusiGod staff authorized to work on your account. Documents are used exclusively to:

  • Verify catalog ownership and rights
  • Support recovery case analysis and society submissions
  • Fulfill legal and contractual obligations

Documents are retained per our Document Retention Policy. Royalty statements are retained for a minimum of 5 years. Signed agreements are retained for 7 years. See Section 8 for full retention schedules.

6. Signatures and Agreement Records

When you sign an agreement or accept terms through the platform, we record:

  • The version of the document you signed
  • The timestamp and method of signature
  • Your IP address and device information at the time of signing
  • A hash of the rendered agreement for integrity verification

These records constitute the binding signature event and are retained as part of our immutable legal audit trail. They are not deletable under standard deletion requests.

7. Audit Findings and Recovery Intelligence

MusiGod generates analytical outputs including audit scores, royalty leakage estimates, recovery probability scores, and recommended actions. These outputs are derived from your catalog data using our internal algorithms. They are:

  • Used solely to conduct recovery operations on your behalf
  • Not shared with third parties except collection societies as necessary for recovery
  • Retained as part of your operational record for dispute resolution and audit defense
Audit findings, recovery estimates, and intelligence outputs are analytical projections. They do not represent guaranteed outcomes and are not shared outside MusiGod's operational systems without your authorization.

9. Your Rights

Subject to our document retention requirements and legal hold obligations, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of data not subject to legal retention requirements
  • Portability — Request export of your catalog metadata in a structured format
  • Restriction — Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

Note: Deletion requests cannot remove data subject to legal holds, active recovery cases, or statutory retention requirements. We will inform you of any applicable restrictions when processing your request.

10. Operational Audit Logs

MusiGod maintains an immutable operational audit log of key platform events including agreement signatures, authorization grants, terms acceptances, and administrative actions. These logs exist to protect both you and MusiGod in the event of disputes and cannot be modified or deleted by any party.

11. IP Address Collection

IP addresses are collected at the following events: registration, login, agreement signing, terms acceptance, and payment initiation. IP data is retained for 1 year for security purposes. At agreement signing, IP data is included in the signature record and retained for the duration of the agreement retention period.

12. Security

MusiGod employs industry-standard security practices including encrypted data storage (Supabase encryption at rest), HTTPS-only data transmission, API key authentication, and role-based access controls. We conduct periodic security reviews of our infrastructure.

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

13. Children's Privacy

MusiGod is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email and posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
