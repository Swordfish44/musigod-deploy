Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

MusiGod Royalties ("MusiGod," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, legal identity information, tax identification numbers, banking details for royalty remittance, and catalog metadata (song titles, recording information, split percentages).

We also collect information automatically when you use our platform, including usage data, log data, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services — including registering your catalog with PROs, DSPs, and international collecting societies; collecting and remitting royalties on your behalf; detecting underpayments and initiating recovery; and communicating with you about your account and registrations.

We use your tax identification information solely for legally required tax reporting and compliance purposes.

Information Sharing

We share your information with third-party services as necessary to provide our services, including performing rights organizations (ASCAP, BMI, SoundExchange, etc.), digital service providers (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, etc.), international collecting societies, payment processors (Stripe, Tipalti), and neighboring rights collection agencies.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with advertisers.

Data Security

We implement cryptographic audit trails on all royalty events and use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. All sensitive financial data is stored with row-level security controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and for a period thereafter as required by law, or as necessary to provide our services and resolve disputes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also request that we restrict processing of your information. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@musigod.com.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our platform and to hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@musigod.com
