Third-Party Registration Fees

This section explains fees you may pay directly to third-party organizations — these are completely separate from your MusiGod subscription fee.

MusiGod subscription fees vs. society registration fees. Your monthly MusiGod subscription pays for our service — the work we do to register, monitor, and recover royalties on your behalf. It does not cover fees charged by performing rights organizations, government agencies, or collecting societies. Those fees, where they exist, are paid by you directly to those organizations.

Fee-bearing registrations and approximate amounts:

  • ASCAP Writer membership — $50 one-time, paid directly to ASCAP
  • ASCAP Publisher registration — $50 one-time, paid directly to ASCAP
  • BMI Publisher registration — $150 one-time, paid directly to BMI
  • PRS for Music (UK) — approximately GBP 100 one-time, paid directly to PRS
  • GEMA (Germany) — approximately EUR 60 per year, paid directly to GEMA
  • Trademark registration (USPTO) — $250–350 per class, paid directly to USPTO
  • State LLC formation — $50–500 depending on your state, paid directly to the state
  • All other registrations MusiGod handles — free

Fees are paid directly to each organization. MusiGod does not collect, hold, or pass through third-party registration fees. When a fee-bearing registration is ready, we will provide you with clear instructions and the exact payment link for that organization. You pay them. We handle everything else.

Fee changes by third parties. MusiGod is not responsible for changes to fees charged by third-party organizations. Society fees can change at any time. We will do our best to keep you informed, but always confirm the current fee directly with the organization before payment.

We notify you before initiating any fee-bearing registration. MusiGod will not submit a registration that requires you to pay a third-party fee without first notifying you of the amount and giving you the opportunity to approve or decline that registration.
