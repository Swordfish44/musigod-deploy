Data Retention Policy

Document Retention Schedule & Legal Hold Framework  ·  MusiGod Publishing Administration Platform

1. Overview

MusiGod retains data for the minimum period necessary to fulfill legal, contractual, and operational obligations. Retention periods are determined by applicable law, contractual commitments, and the operational necessity of maintaining accurate recovery records.

Deletion requests cannot override legal retention requirements, active legal holds, or open recovery case obligations. We will inform you of any applicable restrictions when processing a deletion request.

2. Retention Schedule

Document Category Retention Period Legal Basis Deletable on Request
Signed Publishing Agreements 7 years Michigan contract statute of limitations (6 yr) + 1-year margin No
Recovery Authorization Documents 7 years Legal requirement — financial obligation record No
Uploaded Royalty Statements 5 years Operational — dispute resolution support Yes*
Audit Findings & Reports 7 years Contractual — supports recovery claims No
Recovery Operations Logs Indefinite Operational necessity — immutable audit trail No
Legal Audit Event Logs Indefinite Compliance — immutable legal record No
Artist Profile Data 3 years after inactivity Contractual — subject to legal hold if open cases Yes*
IP Addresses / Session Logs 1 year Security and fraud prevention Yes
Affiliate Commission Records 7 years IRS record-keeping requirements No

* Deletion eligible subject to legal hold review. Deletion blocked if active recovery case exists or legal hold is in place.

3. Legal Holds

MusiGod may place a legal hold on any data category where litigation, regulatory inquiry, or active dispute makes deletion inadvisable. Legal holds override standard retention schedules and deletion requests. We will notify affected artists when a legal hold is placed or lifted on their data where required by applicable law.

4. Immutable Records

The following records are immutable and cannot be modified or deleted by any party, including MusiGod staff:

  • Legal audit event logs (signature events, authorization grants, terms acceptances)
  • Recovery operations logs
  • Agreement signature snapshots

Immutability is enforced at the infrastructure level and exists to protect both artists and MusiGod in the event of disputes.

5. Deletion Requests

To request deletion of eligible data, contact us at support@musigod.com with subject line: DATA DELETION REQUEST — [Your Name]

We will review your request within 30 days and inform you of the outcome, including any portions of your request that cannot be fulfilled due to legal retention requirements.
